What is Snake?

Snake is a video game which has been released back in the 70s in arcades and has became a classic and much loved video game. This game is most loved on the old Nokia mobile phones.

how to play is by controling a long and thin creature resembling a snake which roams freely around on a bordered plane, picking up fruit while avoiding hitting its tail or the walls that surround the playing area. Every time the snake eats a piece of fruit, it will grows longer, making the game increasingly difficult and competitive. The user controls the direction of the snake's head (up, down, left, or right), and the snake's body will follow.

Controls?

All you are relying on for this game are the directional buttons on the demo by clicking top, bottom, left and right. which allows you to control your snake nice and smoothly throughout the demo.


Fruit?

The fruit will be placed randomly around the website for your snake to chase after once your snake gets larger it will become much more difficult to reach to fruit the further you progress.


Time?

There is no time limit throughout the whole game demo the only time when the game is over is when your snake touches its body and shows you your score at the end of the demo.


Availability?

The snake game will be available on Apple and Android devices.
